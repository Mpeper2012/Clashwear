FAQ

How do I pick the right size?

If you’re between sizes, go up. For example, if you’re between Medium and Large, choose Large for extra room. Slides are meant to be easy to slip on and off.

Do all designs have every color?

Yes. Every slide design in our shop can be customized in red, yellow, purple, pink, black, white, grey, orange, blue, and green.

Can I change my order after adding to cart?

Yes. Go to the cart section and remove items or add new ones from the shop with different colors or sizes.
